About Us

At Kayondo Boda Loans, we are committed to helping Ugandans achieve the dream of motorcycle ownership without the burden of paying the full purchase price upfront. Through our simple, flexible, and transparent hire purchase program, riders can acquire a motorcycle and pay for it through affordable weekly installments tailored to their earning capacity.

With installments starting from as low as 60,000 UGX, getting your own boda boda has never been easier. Join thousands of satisfied riders who are already earning, growing, and building a better future with us. Your dream, your pace, our support.
